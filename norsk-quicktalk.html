Norsk QuickTalk

Construction heavy. Daily life ready. Search, audio, icons, and a dash of site slang.
Tip: tap the star to save, the triangle to hear Norwegian (TTS needs a browser voice for nb-NO), and the copy icon to grab text. Items in slang are site-talk—use with a smile.
Norwegian sounds: å ~ "aw" (as in law), ø ~ "uh/eur" (French peur), æ ~ "a" in cat. kj ≈ soft hy, skj/sj ≈ "sh".
Emergency numbers: Police 112, Ambulance 113, Fire 110.
Patterns: Jeg trenger … (I need …) • Hvor er …? (Where is …?) • Kan du …? (Can you …?) • Er det i vater? (Is it level?)
