📍 Bergen Places

Tip: tap the star to save, the triangle to hear pronunciation, and the copy icon to grab text. Items in slang are site-talk—use with a smile. Switch between Norwegian 🇳🇴 and Finnish 🇫🇮 using the language selector.
Norwegian sounds: å ~ "aw" (as in law), ø ~ "uh/eur" (French peur), æ ~ "a" in cat. kj ≈ soft hy, skj/sj ≈ "sh".
Finnish sounds: ä ~ "a" in cat, ö ~ German ö, double vowels are long. All letters pronounced.
Emergency numbers: Norway: Police 112, Ambulance 113, Fire 110. Finland: All emergencies 112.
Patterns: Jeg trenger … (I need …) • Hvor er …? (Where is …?) • Kan du …? (Can you …?) • Er det i vater? (Is it level?)
